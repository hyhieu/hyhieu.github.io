ABOUT

I'm currently a resident at Google Brain. My current projects focus on how to make neural networks capable of understanding combinatorial configurations and perform reasonings on them.

Prior, I received my undergraduate degree at Stanford University. I was lucky to have Professor Chris Manning as my honors thesis advisor.

In Fall 2017, I will join the PhD program at the Language Technology Institute (LTI) of Carnegie Mellon University.

This site is still under heavy constructions. I will update a lot of contents as soon as I have time.
